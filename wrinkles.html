Essentials that should be used for normal skin

Wrinkle prevention and treatment for normal skin include Retinol, which boosts collagen production and smooths fine lines. Hyaluronic Acid keeps the skin hydrated and plump, reducing the appearance of wrinkles. Peptides strengthen the skin structure, while Vitamin C provides antioxidant protection to prevent premature aging. Using a retinol-based night cream and a hydrating serum during the day helps maintain youthful skin.

Essentials that should be used for Dry skin

Wrinkle-prone dry skin needs intense hydration and barrier repair. Hyaluronic Acid and Glycerin deeply hydrate and plump the skin, reducing fine lines. Ceramides restore the skin’s protective barrier, preventing moisture loss. Squalane and Shea Butter nourish and smooth the skin, while Bakuchiol provides a gentle, non-irritating alternative to retinol. A rich anti-aging cream with hydrating and nourishing ingredients keeps dry skin firm and youthful.

Essentials that should be used for oily skin

Oily skin benefits from lightweight, anti-aging treatments. Retinol boosts cell turnover and smooths wrinkles without making skin greasy. Niacinamide helps control oil production while improving skin elasticity. Green Tea Extract provides antioxidants to fight premature aging. Aloe Vera soothes and hydrates without clogging pores. A lightweight, oil-free retinol serum at night and a niacinamide-based moisturizer during the day help keep wrinkles at bay.

Essentials that should be used for combination skin

Combination skin requires balanced hydration and anti-aging care. Hyaluronic Acid hydrates dry areas, while Retinol smooths fine lines and wrinkles. Peptides firm the skin and improve elasticity. Vitamin E provides antioxidant protection to prevent aging. Using a lightweight hydrating serum in the morning and a retinol-infused night cream helps reduce wrinkles while maintaining skin balance.

Essentials that should be used for sensitive skin

Sensitive skin needs gentle anti-aging ingredients that don’t cause irritation. Bakuchiol is a plant-based alternative to retinol that smooths fine lines without sensitivity. Centella Asiatica soothes inflammation and strengthens the skin barrier. Ceramides help maintain moisture and reduce wrinkles. Oat Extract calms and protects delicate skin. A fragrance-free anti-aging cream with soothing and hydrating ingredients works best for wrinkle prevention in sensitive skin.
