Essentials that should be present in your cleanser

A cleanser for normal skin should be gentle, hydrating, and pH-balanced (5.5-6.5) to maintain the skin’s natural barrier without stripping moisture. Key ingredients include hydrators like glycerin, hyaluronic acid, and aloe vera, gentle surfactants like cocamidopropyl betaine, and antioxidants such as vitamin C and green tea extract for protection. It should be free from sulfates, alcohol, artificial fragrances, and harsh chemicals to prevent irritation. The best cleanser types for normal skin include gel, cream, or mild foaming cleansers that effectively cleanse while keeping the skin balanced and healthy.

Essentials that should be present in your Toner

A toner for normal skin should be hydrating, balancing, and refreshing, helping to maintain the skin’s natural moisture and pH levels. It should contain hydrating agents like Aloe Vera, Hyaluronic Acid, and Cucumber Extract to keep the skin plump and nourished, along with gentle astringents such as Rose Water and Green Tea Extract to tone and refine pores. Additionally, pH balancers like Apple Cider Vinegar (diluted) or Alcohol-Free Witch Hazel help maintain skin health, while antioxidants like Vitamin C and Vitamin E protect against environmental damage. To prevent dryness or irritation, it’s best to choose an alcohol-free formula for a natural, glowing complexion.

Essentials that should be present in your serum

A serum for normal skin should be hydrating, nourishing, and protective, containing ingredients like Hyaluronic Acid, Glycerin, and Aloe Vera for hydration, Vitamin C and Niacinamide for brightening, Green Tea Extract and Vitamin E for antioxidant protection, and Peptides, Ceramides, and Squalane to strengthen the skin barrier, while gentle exfoliants like Lactic Acid promote a healthy glow—all in a lightweight, non-greasy, and alcohol-free formula.

Essentials that should be present in your moisturizer

A moisturizer for normal skin should provide hydration, nourishment, and protection, containing Hyaluronic Acid, Glycerin, and Aloe Vera for moisture, Squalane, Jojoba Oil, and Shea Butter for nourishment, Ceramides and Peptides to strengthen the skin barrier, and Vitamin E, Green Tea Extract, and Niacinamide for antioxidant protection, while a lightweight, non-greasy, and fast-absorbing formula with SPF 30+ (for daytime use) helps maintain a smooth, radiant, and healthy complexion.

Essentials that should be present in your sunscreen

A sunscreen for normal skin should offer broad-spectrum protection (UVA & UVB) while keeping the skin hydrated, lightweight, and non-greasy. It should contain SPF 30+ or higher for daily use, with ingredients like Zinc Oxide or Titanium Dioxide for mineral protection, or Avobenzone, Octocrylene, and Tinosorb for chemical protection. Hydrating agents like Hyaluronic Acid and Glycerin help maintain moisture, while antioxidants such as Vitamin E, Green Tea Extract, and Niacinamide protect against environmental damage. A non-comedogenic, fragrance-free, and sweat-resistant formula is ideal for all-day wear, ensuring a healthy, glowing complexion.
