Essentials that should be present in your cleanser

A cleanser for sensitive skin should be gentle, fragrance-free, and soothing, designed to cleanse without causing irritation. It should include hydrating ingredients like Glycerin, Hyaluronic Acid, and Aloe Vera to maintain moisture, anti-inflammatory agents like Chamomile Extract and Centella Asiatica to calm redness, and barrier-strengthening ingredients like Ceramides and Oat Extract to protect the skin. A sulfate-free, pH-balanced, and non-foaming formula is ideal to prevent dryness and irritation while keeping the skin clean and comfortable.

Essentials that should be present in your sunscreen

A sunscreen for sensitive skin should be gentle, protective, and soothing, offering broad-spectrum UVA & UVB protection with SPF 30+ or higher. It should contain mineral filters like Zinc Oxide and Titanium Dioxide, which are less likely to cause irritation compared to chemical filters. Soothing ingredients such as Aloe Vera, Centella Asiatica, Green Tea Extract, and Oat Extract help calm the skin, while hydrators like Hyaluronic Acid and Glycerin maintain moisture. A fragrance-free, alcohol-free, non-comedogenic, and hypoallergenic formula is essential to prevent irritation and keep sensitive skin comfortable and protected throughout the day.
