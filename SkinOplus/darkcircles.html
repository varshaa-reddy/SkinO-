Essentials that should be used for normal skin

Dark circles in normal skin can be treated with Vitamin C to brighten the under-eye area and reduce pigmentation. Caffeine helps improve circulation and reduce puffiness. Hyaluronic Acid keeps the under-eye area hydrated and plump, preventing fine lines. Peptides strengthen the skin and improve elasticity. Using a Vitamin C or caffeine-based eye cream in the morning and a peptide-rich hydrating cream at night helps reduce dark circles effectively.

Essentials that should be used for Dry skin

Dark circles in dry skin need deep hydration and nourishment. Hyaluronic Acid and Glycerin provide long-lasting moisture. Almond Oil and Vitamin E deeply nourish and repair the delicate under-eye skin. Shea Butter prevents dryness and reduces fine lines. Licorice Extract brightens pigmentation gently. A rich eye cream with hydrating and nourishing ingredients, applied morning and night, helps improve dark circles and dryness.

Essentials that should be used for oily skin

Oily skin benefits from lightweight yet effective treatments. Caffeine reduces puffiness and dark circles by improving blood circulation. Niacinamide helps lighten pigmentation without clogging pores. Aloe Vera soothes and hydrates without making the skin greasy. Green Tea Extract provides antioxidants to reduce dark circles caused by stress and fatigue. A lightweight gel-based eye cream with caffeine and niacinamide in the morning, followed by aloe vera at night, keeps the under-eye area bright and fresh.

Essentials that should be used for combination skin

Combination skin needs balanced hydration and targeted treatment. Hyaluronic Acid hydrates the dry areas while keeping the skin plump. Caffeine reduces puffiness and dark circles. Peptides help firm and strengthen the delicate under-eye skin. Chamomile Extract soothes and calms any irritation. A combination of caffeine-based treatment in the morning and peptides at night helps improve dark circles without disrupting skin balance.

Essentials that should be used for sensitive skin

Sensitive skin requires gentle, soothing ingredients. Aloe Vera calms irritation and provides hydration. Centella Asiatica strengthens the skin barrier and reduces redness. Rosehip Oil brightens dark circles without causing irritation. Oat Extract soothes and protects delicate under-eye skin. A fragrance-free, hypoallergenic eye cream with soothing ingredients, applied twice daily, helps reduce dark circles while keeping the skin calm and healthy.
