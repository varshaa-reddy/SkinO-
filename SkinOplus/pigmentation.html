Essentials that should be used for normal skin

To reduce pigmentation on normal skin, Vitamin C helps brighten and even out skin tone, while Niacinamide works to reduce excess melanin production. Alpha Arbutin gently lightens dark patches and hyperpigmentation. Licorice Extract soothes and enhances skin radiance. A combination of Vitamin C in the morning and alpha arbutin or niacinamide at night, along with daily sunscreen, prevents further pigmentation.

Essentials that should be used for Dry skin

Pigmentation in dry skin should be treated with hydrating and repairing ingredients. Hyaluronic Acid ensures the skin remains plump and hydrated while supporting healing. Lactic Acid, a mild AHA, gently exfoliates to fade pigmentation without irritation. Vitamin E nourishes and protects the skin from environmental damage. Rosehip Oil boosts skin renewal and reduces dark patches. A Vitamin C serum in the morning and a mild lactic acid exfoliant at night, followed by a nourishing moisturizer, works best.

Essentials that should be used for oily skin

Oily skin needs lightweight but effective ingredients to reduce pigmentation. Niacinamide regulates oil production and helps fade discoloration. Kojic Acid inhibits melanin production, reducing hyperpigmentation. Salicylic Acid exfoliates dead skin and improves texture, helping to clear post-acne marks. Green Tea Extract provides antioxidant protection and calms inflammation. A niacinamide serum in the morning and a salicylic acid or kojic acid treatment at night, paired with sunscreen, gives visible results.

Essentials that should be used for combination skin

Combination skin benefits from Vitamin C, which brightens and evens out skin tone. Mandelic Acid is a gentle exfoliant that works on both oily and dry areas without irritation. Azelaic Acid reduces inflammation and pigmentation while preventing breakouts. Licorice Extract helps soothe and fade discoloration. Using a Vitamin C serum in the morning and an azelaic or mandelic acid treatment at night ensures balanced skin tone correction.

Essentials that should be used for sensitive skin

Pigmentation on sensitive skin requires gentle but effective ingredients. Azelaic Acid helps fade dark spots while calming redness. Centella Asiatica soothes irritation and strengthens the skin barrier. Oat Extract provides hydration and reduces inflammation. Chamomile Extract helps in calming the skin and reducing pigmentation caused by irritation. A mild azelaic acid treatment at night and a calming, hydrating serum in the morning, combined with SPF, protects and treats pigmentation safely.
